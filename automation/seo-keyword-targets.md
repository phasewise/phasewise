# Phasewise — Master SEO Keyword Target List

40+ keywords organized by topic cluster. Designed to build topical authority — Google rewards sites that cover a topic comprehensively, not just one article on the topic.

Each cluster has a mix of:
- **Top-of-funnel** (TOFU) — informational searches, wide audience, funnel-top
- **Mid-funnel** (MOFU) — comparative/evaluative searches, shopping intent
- **Bottom-of-funnel** (BOFU) — commercial intent, ready to buy

The n8n pipeline picks one keyword at a time from this list, generates an article, and moves to the next. Rotate through clusters rather than exhausting one at a time — Google likes to see consistent new content across topical areas.

**Cadence recommendation:** 2-3 articles per week = ~100-150 new articles in year one. That's when compounding SEO authority starts to dominate.

## How to find more keywords later

When this list is exhausted, top sources for LA-specific keyword research:

1. **Google "People Also Ask" boxes** for any existing article topic
2. **Google Search Console** once traffic starts — shows actual queries driving impressions
3. **Reddit** r/LandscapeArchitecture, r/architecture (questions = keyword ideas)
4. **ASLA forums** and industry publications
5. **Competitor analysis** — Monograph, Asana, Harvest, QuickBooks blog post titles
6. **Industry podcasts** — LA industry podcast episode titles are great keyword seeds

Once the pipeline is running, add 10-20 new keywords per month to keep it fed.
