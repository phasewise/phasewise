## Common mistakes to avoid

- **Don't overstate features.** If Phasewise doesn't have a feature, uncheck that box. A listing that overpromises generates one-star reviews.
- **Don't use stock marketing fluff.** Directory users are comparison shoppers — specificity wins.
- **Don't skip screenshots.** Every directory has a screenshot upload. Use the hero mockup from the landing page + 2-3 clean shots of the dashboard, timesheet, and submittal log. Dark + clean mockups outperform real data.
- **Don't claim Enterprise features** (SSO, SCIM, advanced permissions, etc.) that aren't built.
- **Don't link to your Twitter/LinkedIn unless the accounts are actively populated** — empty social profiles hurt credibility.
