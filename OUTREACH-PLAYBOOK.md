# Phasewise Outreach Playbook

The product is feature-complete. Customers are now the only thing standing between you and revenue. This is the cold-outreach play to land the first 3 paying firms.

**Goal for next 30 days:** 3 firms in 14-day trial, 1 converted to paying.

---

## The math

Cold-email response rates for B2B SaaS targeting niche verticals: **5–15% reply rate**, **1–3% trial conversion** when the message is specific and the targeting is right.

To net 3 trials → send ~150 well-targeted emails over 30 days = ~5/day.

---

## Who to target

Three concentric circles, work from inside out:

### Inner circle (highest hit rate, ~15-25 firms)

People you have at least one degree of separation from via Caltrans, ASLA, or California licensure boards. Specifically:

- **Former Caltrans LAs in private practice.** You can name them.
- **California Site Improvement District contacts** you've worked with.
- **ASLA California Sierra / Northern / Southern chapter members** with a published practice.
- **Firms that bid on Caltrans contracts** (public records).

Send a personalized email that opens with the specific connection. Conversion: 30–50%.

### Middle circle (~50-100 firms)

California LA firms with 3-15 staff, founded 2015+, no explicit "we use Monograph" mention on their site. Sweet spot: large enough to need a tool, small enough to not have entrenched workflows.

Find them via:
- ASLA California member directory
- California Architects Board → Landscape Architects search by city
- Google Maps "landscape architecture firm" filtered to California cities
- LinkedIn "Founder + Landscape Architect + California" filter

Personalization required but lighter. Conversion: 5-10%.

### Outer circle (rest of US, 200+)

Any firm in a major metro outside CA. Less personalization, more volume. Conversion: 2-5%.

**Order of operations: inner first, middle second, outer only when first two are exhausted.** The product is built for California (MWELO is CA-specific), so CA firms convert at 3-5x other states.

## Personalization rules (non-negotiable)

Cold emails without personalization land in spam folders or auto-trash piles. **Three minutes of personalization per email** beats the volume of unpersonalized blasts.

- Read the firm's "About" page. Note founding year, principal name, geographic focus.
- Pick **one specific project** from their portfolio. Reference it in the opener.
- Look up the principal's LinkedIn. Note any shared connections (Caltrans, ASLA, California state university LA programs).
- Use the principal's first name in the opener. **Never** "Dear Sir/Madam" or "Hi there".

If you don't know the firm well enough to personalize, don't email yet. Move them to a "research more" list.

## Sending hygiene

- **From address:** kevin@phasewise.io (Google Workspace mailbox is set up).
- **Send 5/day, M-Th, 8-10am Pacific.** Friday afternoons get ignored.
- **Track replies.** Even "not interested" is a tag — don't re-email them.
- **No tracking pixels.** Most LA firms run Apple Mail / Outlook with image-blocking; pixels break trust without giving you data.
- **Plain text > HTML.** Looks like a real human email, lands in primary inbox more often.
- **Never use mail-merge syntax in the actual sent email.** Manual personalization or use a tool like Apollo / Hunter that does merge cleanly.

## Day-1 / Week-1 plan

**Today:**
1. Build the inner-circle list (15-25 firms). Spend 2-3 hours on this. It's the highest-leverage activity in this entire playbook.
2. Send 5 emails using Template 1 to the warmest contacts.
3. Add yourself to ASLA California chapter if not already a member ($150/yr — pays for itself with one trial signup).

**This week:**
4. Send 5 inner-circle emails per day, M-Th = 20 total.
5. Add 30-50 middle-circle prospects to the spreadsheet.
6. Friday: review inner-circle response rate. If <20%, the personalization isn't tight enough — review and re-tune.

**Week 2:**
7. Follow-up #1 sweep on inner-circle non-responders.
8. Start middle-circle outreach with Template 2.
9. Aim for 25 emails total (15 follow-ups + 10 new middle-circle).

**Week 3-4:**
10. Breakup emails to the persistent non-responders.
11. Continue middle-circle outreach at 5/day.
12. Goal: 3 trial signups by end of week 4.

## What "winning" looks like in 30 days

- 100-150 emails sent across all three waves
- 10-25 replies (10-15% reply rate is healthy for a niche-vertical cold email)
- 3-5 booked walkthroughs (15-min screen shares)
- 3 trial signups
- 1 paid conversion (starting MRR: $99-$199)

If you're at 50-emails sent / 0 replies on day 14, the targeting or personalization is off — pause and diagnose, don't push more volume.
