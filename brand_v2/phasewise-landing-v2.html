Features

Everything your firm needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Purpose-built for the way landscape architecture firms actually operate — from first client meeting to final punchlist.

Phase-Based Projects
SD → DD → CDs → CA → Closeout. Your phases are already here. New projects start in the right structure automatically.
Real-Time Budget Tracking
Know exactly how much is spent per project and per phase the moment a timesheet is submitted. Alerts before you go over.
Time Tracking That Fits
Weekly timesheet grid by project and phase. Mobile-friendly for field and remote staff. Takes less than two minutes to complete.

Track submittals, RFIs, and half-ball with automated reminders and a searchable log. Full history, always current.
Plant Schedule Manager
Live plant database with substitution tracking and client-approval workflows. Export directly to CAD-compatible format.
Compliance Tracker
MWELO water budgets, LEED credits, privacy screening — all in one place with zero redundancy across projects.
